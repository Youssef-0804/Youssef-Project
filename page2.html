What is a Scientific book

What is the meaning of scientific book? A science book is a work of nonfiction, usually written by a scientist, researcher,
or professor like Stephen Hawking (A Brief History of Time),
or sometimes by a non-scientist such as Bill Bryson (A Short History of Nearly Everything).
